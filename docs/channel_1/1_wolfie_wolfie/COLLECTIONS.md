# Collection Definitions (WOLFIE Authoritative Layer)

## WHO
- **Meaning**: People, agents, teams, or organizations.  
- **Content**: Maintainer info, contact routes, roles/responsibilities.

## WHAT
- **Meaning**: Description of the document or component itself.  
- **Content**: Overview, feature summary, release scope.

## WHERE
- **Meaning**: Physical or digital locations relevant to the content.  
- **Content**: Paths, environments, deployment targets, URLs.

## WHEN
- **Meaning**: Timelines, milestones, or release dates.  
- **Content**: Changelog entries, schedules, cadence notes.

## WHY
- **Meaning**: Purpose, motivation, or strategic context.  
- **Content**: Problem statements, goals, decision rationale.

## HOW
- **Meaning**: Implementation steps, workflows, validation.  
- **Content**: Procedures, scripts, testing plans, automation hooks.

## HELP
- **Meaning**: Support channels, FAQs, escalation routes.  
- **Content**: Contact info, troubleshooting tips, community links.
